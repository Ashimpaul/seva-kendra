What is a Trademark?

A trademark is your business's unique signature - whether it's a symbol, name, or design that makes your brand stand out. It's your exclusive way of identifying your products or services in the market.

Types of Trademarks

Word Marks

Names or phrases (e.g., "Apple," "Nike")

Device Marks

Symbols or designs (e.g., Nike swoosh)

Combination Marks

Combined words and designs
