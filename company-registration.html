Documents Required

For Directors

  • Passport Size Photograph
  • Address Proof (Bank statement/Electricity bill - not more than 2 months old)
  • PAN Card and Aadhar Card
  • Identity Proof (Driving license/Voter ID/Passport)
  • Email ID and Mobile Number
  • Digital Signature Certificate
  • DIR-2 Form

For Office & Company

  • Registered Office Address Proof (Electricity/Telephone/Gas Bill - not more than 2 months old)
  • Rent Agreement on Rs. 100 stamp paper with Notary (Company name with complete address)
  • NOC on Rs. 100 stamp paper (Company name must be mentioned)
  • Board Resolution authorising investment
