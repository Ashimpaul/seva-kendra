Frequently Asked Questions

Who needs GST registration?

Businesses with turnover exceeding ₹40 lakhs (goods) or ₹20 lakhs (services) must register for GST.

What are the documents required for GST registration?

PAN, Aadhaar, business registration documents, bank account details, and property documents are required.

How long does GST registration take?

Usually 3-7 working days after submission of all required documents.
