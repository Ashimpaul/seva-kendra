Company Registration in India

Complete guide on LLP, OPC, Partnership, Nidhi, Private & Section-8 companies

Steps to Register a Company

  • ✔ Choose a unique company name
  • ✔ Apply for a Digital Signature Certificate (DSC)
  • ✔ Obtain a Director Identification Number (DIN)
  • ✔ Prepare and submit required documents
  • ✔ File the incorporation application
  • ✔ Receive the Certificate of Incorporation

Types of Companies in India
