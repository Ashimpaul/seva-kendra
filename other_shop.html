Registration Under Shop & Establishment Act

The Shop and Establishment Act (S&E Act) regulates working conditions for employees in businesses. All shops meeting specific criteria must register.

  • Legal compliance to avoid penalties.
  • Better employee relations and work environment.
  • Enhanced brand image and credibility.

Required Documents

  • Aadhar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Trade Name
  • Rental Agreement (if applicable)

Partnership Registration

A partnership is a business model where two or more individuals collaborate, sharing profits as per an agreed ratio. Governed by the Indian Partnership Act of 1932.

Required Documents

  • GST Certificate
  • MSME Registration
  • Partnership Deed
  • PAN & Aadhar of Partners
